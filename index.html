About Anamitra

Anamitra Chatterjee, Accredited Executive Coach & Faculty has had education and experience
of 25+ years in India & UK across Deutsche Bank and PwC, Hult Ashridge UK & IIMs in India.

Anamitra Chatterjee is an Accredited Executive Coach (EMCC). He has 12+ years of independent practice in Leadership Development & Executive Coaching. He has coached over 230 senior leaders and 2600 hours. As Faculty, he designs and delivers executive education programmes on consulting skills, organization & leadership development. He has delivered training to over 25 clients and 4000 managers.
