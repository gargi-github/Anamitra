Executive Education

Anamitra Chatterjee, Accredited Executive Coach & Faculty has had education and experience
of 25+ years in India & UK across Deutsche Bank and PwC, Hult Ashridge UK & IIMs in India.

As Faculty, he designs and delivers executive education programmes on organization & leadership development.
He has delivered training to over 25 clients and 4000 managers.

Anamitra Chatterjee, Accredited Executive Coach (EMCC) holds a Master’s degree, MSc Masters in Executive
Coaching (AMEC) from Hult Ashridge Centre for Coaching (ACC), UK.

He underwent the Leadership Development Program (LDP), Center for Creative Leadership (CCL) Brussels.
Anamitra has 12+ years of work experience in executive education: INSEAD, Hult Ashridge Executive Education UK & IIMs in India.

Anamitra’s legacy in consulting skills

Consulting skills executive education has been his legacy. He was the Lead Trainer of the flagship management consulting course at PwC globally: MCS Level 1 (GEM Level 1). Post PwC, he used his experience and knowledge to create a new Strategy elective on Consulting skills at IIMs in India.

As Faculty, he has delivered Consulting skills executive education across IIMs and corporate clients.
He has delivered Consulting skills courses about 50 times to over 2000 participants.

 A typical consulting skills course takes about 24 learning hours, spread over several sessions in
a full week with case study and assignments dovetailed within the learning week.

Anamitra believes: Consulting takes the word with (and not to). A Consultant "consults with" clients.

Anamitra has 20+ years of experience in facilitation and teaching of Consulting skills corporate education. He uses his experiences and skills in experiential learning and role-playing while ‘’consulting with consultants’’ to develop in them skillful and flawless consulting skills. He is widely acknowledged as a subject matter expert in executive education and corporate training of this niche area, Consulting Skills.
