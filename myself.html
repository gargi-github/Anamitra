Anamitra Chatterjee

Anamitra Chatterjee, Accredited Executive Coach (EMCC) & Faculty has had education and experience
of 25+ years in India & UK across Deutsche Bank and PwC, Hult Ashridge UK & IIMs in India.

Anamitra Chatterjee is an Accredited Executive Coach (EMCC). He has 12+ years of independent practice in Leadership Development & Executive Coaching. He has coached over 230 senior leaders and 2600 hours. As Faculty, he designs and delivers executive education programmes on consulting skills, organizational & leadership development. He has delivered training to over 25 clients and 4000 managers.

Anamitra Chatterjee, holds two post-graduate qualifications: MBA (PGDM) from Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Calcutta &
PGD from London School of Economics & Political Science (LSE) and a Master's degree, MSc Masters in Executive Coaching (AMEC) from
Hult Ashridge UK. He studied on a British Chevening scholarship at LSE, UK. He got a Distinction in BSc - Economics from Presidency College.

He is a certified practitioner of psychometrics: Hogan & LVI360, Belbin, FIRO-B & MBTI.
Anamitra underwent the World Class Mentoring (Elite Sports & Business) Programme from Ashtridge UK.
